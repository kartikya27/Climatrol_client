About us

Who we are

Climatrol Corporation is privately held Indian company with specialization in design, development & manufacturing of Refrigeration Equipments to meet different demand & application of our customers. Climatrol typically undertakes Cold Room & Frozen Room projects with Puf Panels, Condensing Units or Rack System. In house manufacturing capability of Puf Panels & Refrigeration Systems gives us an edge in highly competitive market.
